[A more detailed guide in game's how to play]

Prevent ghost from entering blue portal.
Lose health when ghost enters blue portal, lose
if health reaches 0
Kill all ghost in wave to proceed to next wave
Win the level after clearing all wave

Click turrets button and place it on empty spot 
in grid, place on path to change the pathing.

Click on turrets placed to see range of turrets 
and display upgrade and sell options at right 
side of screen.

Click on swap button to change to power up 
menu

Click on convert button at top right corner
to convert Phantom Quartz to Gold Quartz
(1000 Phantom Quartz for 10 Gold Quartz)

Battlefield effects in game colour green are 
postive effect for player and colour red effects
are negative effect on player
